Santa Fe Venture Studio Santa Fe Venture Studio co-founds startups with tenacious, traction-obsessed partners. We hunt in crypto, fintech, commerce and content. We like capital-light, employee-light businesses where we can simplify a complex problem through unfair advantages we have through team, network, and a key insight. - We like to find ways to test the hypothesis in the fastest way with the least effort. - We don't invest outside capital. We experiment with small no salary team of world-class founders without full-time commitment.
